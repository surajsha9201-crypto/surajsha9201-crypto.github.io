Mobile Charging Problem Fix

Charging problems are very common in smartphones. Sometimes the phone charges slowly, disconnects automatically, or does not charge at all.

Main Causes of Charging Problems

  • Dirty charging port
  • Damaged USB cable
  • Faulty charger adapter
  • Charging IC problem
  • Water damage inside phone

Easy Solutions at Home

First clean the charging port carefully using a soft brush. Try another original charger and cable. Restart the phone and check software updates.

Signs of Hardware Damage

If the phone only charges at one angle, or charging stops repeatedly, then the charging connector or motherboard may be damaged.
