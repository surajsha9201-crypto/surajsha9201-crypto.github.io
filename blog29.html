Mobile Water Damage Solution

Water damage is one of the most dangerous problems for smartphones. Many phones stop working properly after falling into water. Quick action can save your device from permanent damage.

What Happens After Water Damage?

  • Phone may switch off automatically
  • Display can become black or flicker
  • Speaker and microphone may stop working
  • Charging problem may appear
  • Motherboard short circuit can happen

What To Do Immediately

Switch off the phone immediately and remove SIM card and memory card. Do not charge the phone after water damage. Keep the phone dry and avoid using hair dryer with high heat.

Common Mistakes

Many people try charging the phone immediately after water exposure. This can create short circuits inside the motherboard and increase damage.
