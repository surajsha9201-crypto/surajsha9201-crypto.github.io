Mobile Battery Fast Drain Problem Solution

Nowadays many smartphone users face battery draining problems. If your phone battery goes from 100% to 20% very quickly, then this article will help you.

Main Reasons of Battery Drain

  • Background apps running continuously
  • Damaged charging IC
  • Weak battery health
  • Overheating problem
  • High brightness and gaming usage

How to Fix Battery Drain Issue

First check battery health from phone settings. Remove unnecessary apps and clear cache regularly. Always use original charger for charging. Avoid using phone while charging.

When Battery Replacement is Needed

If the phone heats too much or battery percentage drops suddenly, then battery replacement may be required. A damaged battery can also affect phone performance.
