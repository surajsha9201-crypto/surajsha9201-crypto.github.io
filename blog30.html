Display Line Problem Solution

Display line problems are very common in smartphones. Sometimes green, pink or white lines appear on the screen. This issue can make the phone difficult to use.

Main Causes of Display Line Problem

  • Phone falling on hard surface
  • Water damage inside display section
  • Display connector becoming loose
  • Damaged AMOLED or LCD panel
  • Motherboard display IC issue

How to Fix Display Line Issues

Restart the phone and check software updates first. If lines still appear, then the display or connector may need checking. Avoid pressing the screen hard because it can increase damage.

When Display Replacement is Needed

If lines increase continuously or touch stops working, then display replacement may be required. Using original quality display improves performance and durability.
